Severity Assessment

Functionality Affected

Which features or functionalities of the SaaS product are impacted by the risk? Risks that hinder core functionalities may be considered more severe.

Frequency of Occurrence

How often does the risk occur or affect the customer? Risks that happen frequently might be more severe as they can cause ongoing disruptions.

Workarounds Available

If there are no viable workarounds for the risk, it may be considered more severe, as it leaves the customer with no immediate solution.

Number of Affected Users

If the risk affects a large number of users or crucial stakeholders, its severity may increase.
